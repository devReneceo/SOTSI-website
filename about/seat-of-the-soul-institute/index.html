About the institute

The Seat of the Soul Institute

A calm, credible home for spiritual growth. This page carries the same content architecture as the live site, but expressed through our quieter and more premium editorial system.

Get Started Read the foundation →
Founded

Co-founded by Gary Zukav and Linda Francis in 1999.

Core idea

Creating authentic power through the alignment of personality and soul.

Purpose

Support people in making conscious choices rooted in love rather than fear.

Our inspiration

A transformation in consciousness is already underway.

The live page speaks about human evolution requiring spiritual growth and spiritual growth requiring conscious choice. We are keeping that structure, but giving it more space, air, and visual dignity.

We frame the institute as a place for people who sense that inner development is not a side practice, but the center of a meaningful life. The message is calm, not pushy. It invites reflection and responsibility.

Rather than sounding like a course funnel, the page presents the institute as a long-form home for intentional growth, emotional awareness, intuition, and spiritual partnership.

Our mission

Meaning and purpose, creativity and health, joy and love.

The mission section from the live page is one of the strongest content anchors we have. Here it becomes the emotional center of the page instead of just another block of copy.

The institute exists to assist people across the world in creating authentic power. In practical terms, that means helping them grow emotional awareness, make responsible choices, trust intuition, and build spiritual partnerships between equals.

Emotional awareness

Learning to recognize what fear and love feel like in the body, in relationships, and in everyday decisions.

Responsible choice

Moving from reactive patterns to conscious action that reflects the values of the soul.

Spiritual partnership

Relationships between equals created for the purpose of growth, healing, and mutual evolution.

Our means

Programs and tools that return people to conscious choice.

This is where we can later connect the About page to pathways like courses, membership, community, and newsletter without making the page feel promotional. It becomes a bridge into the larger ecosystem.

Values and vision

Harmony, cooperation, sharing, and reverence for Life.

The live site’s values section is short but important. Here it becomes a more cinematic pause before the closing CTA, while the vision expands toward the Universal Human idea already present in SOTSI’s language.

We envision a world of people whose allegiance is to Life itself, beyond nation, race, sex, religion, culture, or economic circumstance.

Adapted from the live About SOTS page

Primary role

Clarify what the institute is, why it exists, and what kind of transformation it supports.

Conversion role

Prepare the visitor for a Get Started action without collapsing the page into direct-response language.

Content note

This page is already ready for future links into courses, membership, and resources when the IA is fully wired.
